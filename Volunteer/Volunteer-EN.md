# Volunteer Agreement

**Asociación Nobodies Collective**

---

\*In the event of any discrepancy between the Spanish version and the English version, the Spanish version shall prevail.

---

## 1. Introduction

This Volunteer Agreement ("Agreement") sets out the terms that apply to all individuals who participate in, contribute to, or collaborate with Asociación Nobodies Collective ("the Association") in any capacity — whether as a Member (*Asociado*), Collaborator (*Colaborador*), or in any other volunteer role.

By completing the Association's onboarding process, the Volunteer acknowledges and accepts the terms set out below.

## 2. Definitions

- **Association** — Asociación Nobodies Collective, a Spanish association (*asociación*) registered under Spanish Organic Law 1/2002.
- **Volunteer** — Any individual who participates in, contributes to, or collaborates with the Association in any capacity.
- **Contribution** — Any work created during or in connection with the Volunteer's participation, including but not limited to: documents, artwork, code, photography, video, audio, training materials, and other creative or intellectual output.
- **Statutes** — The governing bylaws of the Association (*Estatutos*), as approved and amended from time to time.

## 3. Acceptance of Governance Documents

The Volunteer acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to comply with the **Statutes (*Estatutos*)** of the Association — the bylaws governing the Association's structure, membership, rights, duties, and disciplinary procedures. The current version is published at [github.com/nobodies-collective/legal](https://github.com/nobodies-collective/legal).

The Volunteer further agrees to comply with applicable Association rules, policies, and guidelines as communicated to them during their participation. The Association will make reasonable efforts to ensure that such rules are accessible to participants before they take effect.

The Volunteer accepts that the Statutes and other governance documents may be amended from time to time through the procedures established in the Statutes, and agrees to comply with such amendments.

## 4. Data Protection and GDPR

### 4.1 Data Processing

The Association processes personal data for the following purposes:

- Managing the relationship between the Association and the Volunteer
- Maintaining the registers of members and collaborators as required by Spanish law
- Communicating Association activities, events, and updates
- Fulfilling legal and statutory obligations

### 4.2 Legal Basis

Personal data is processed on the basis of the Volunteer's consent and the Association's legitimate interest in managing its activities, in compliance with:

- The General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR")
- Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 on Personal Data Protection and Digital Rights ("LOPD-GDD")

### 4.3 Data Sharing

Personal data will not be sold or shared for purposes unrelated to the Association's activities.

The Association may use third-party service providers (such as email platforms, cloud storage, and communication tools) to carry out its activities. These providers may process personal data on the Association's behalf and solely for the purposes described in this Agreement. The Association will take reasonable steps to ensure that such providers offer adequate data protection safeguards.

Personal data may also be disclosed where required by law or necessary to fulfil the Association's legal obligations.

### 4.4 Volunteer Rights

The Volunteer may exercise the following rights at any time by contacting the Board of Directors at **board@nobodies.team**:

- **Access** — Request a copy of personal data held
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion of personal data
- **Objection** — Object to processing of personal data
- **Restriction** — Request limitation of data processing
- **Portability** — Receive personal data in a structured, machine-readable format

### 4.5 Consent

By completing the Association's onboarding process, the Volunteer provides express consent for the Association to process their personal data for the purposes described above.

## 5. Contributors Agreement

### 5.1 Scope

This section applies to all Contributions made by the Volunteer during or in connection with their participation in the Association.

### 5.2 Ownership

The Volunteer retains full ownership of all Contributions. Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of intellectual property from the Volunteer to the Association.

### 5.3 License Grant

The Volunteer grants the Association a **perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free, non-exclusive license** to use, reproduce, modify, distribute, and publicly display all Contributions under the terms of the **Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International License** (CC BY-SA 4.0).

The full license text is available at: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/legalcode

### 5.4 Derivative Works

Any derivative works based on the Volunteer's Contributions must be distributed under the same CC BY-SA 4.0 license or a compatible license.

### 5.5 Representations

The Volunteer represents and warrants that:

1. They have the legal right to grant the license described in Section 5.3.
2. Their Contributions are original work, or they have obtained the necessary permissions for any third-party materials included.
3. Their Contributions do not infringe the intellectual property rights, privacy rights, or any other rights of any third party.

### 5.6 Pre-existing Works

If the Volunteer wishes to contribute pre-existing works under different licensing terms, they must specify those terms in writing to the Board of Directors before making such a contribution.

### 5.7 Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary. The Volunteer is under no obligation to create or submit Contributions.

### 5.8 Survival

The license granted under Section 5.3 survives termination of the Volunteer's participation in the Association, for Contributions made during the period of participation.

## 6. General Terms

### 6.1 Voluntary Nature

The Volunteer's participation is entirely voluntary and does not create an employment relationship, agency, or partnership between the Volunteer and the Association. The Volunteer is not entitled to any compensation, salary, or benefits unless separately agreed in writing.

### 6.2 Disciplinary Procedures

The Volunteer acknowledges that the Association may take disciplinary action in accordance with the procedures established in the Statutes (Article 32). The Volunteer has the right to be heard before any disciplinary measures are adopted.

### 6.3 Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time. The Volunteer may withdraw by providing written notice to the Board of Directors. The Association may terminate the Volunteer's participation in accordance with the grounds and procedures set out in the Statutes (Article 26).

### 6.4 Amendments

The Association reserves the right to amend this Agreement. Material changes will be communicated to the Volunteer, and continued participation after notification constitutes acceptance of the amended terms.

### 6.5 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by Spanish law. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be resolved in accordance with the procedures established in the Statutes and, where necessary, before the competent courts of the Association's registered office.

### 6.6 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
